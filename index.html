Hello, my name is Bryant Lee and I am a current freshman in the pre-business program at the University of California - Riverside. I am currently looking into concentrating in marketing.

I am interested in marketing because of how creative you can get within the industry. I have enjoyed being creative and trying out different things during high school. I have took courses in graphic design, ceramics, and other types of art.

I have worked in the fast food industry as well as volunteering at multiple establishments. I have also been treasurer and president of youth groups. While working at In-N-Out Burger, I had to do multiple different tasks. I started out with prepping vegetables and then working in the dining room. I also have experience taking orders in both the front of house and in the drive-thru. Working all of these different positions has taught me to be able to adapt to different situations and learning different skills. I volunteered at the Castro Valley Library running game days for younger children during the summer. During the other months of the year, I shelved books, did inventory, and logged how frequently each book or magazine was being checked out. Another place I volunteered at was the East Bay SPCA. At this establishment I did lots of tasks involving rescue animals. I spent most of my time making treats for the dog and cats, decorating the walls for seasonal holidays, and cleaning the kennels. I also took the dogs for walks and restocked the shelves with incoming shipments of food, treats, and other supplies. I have also held leadership positions in my local youth group. I started out as the treasurer for the Oakland Junior Young Buddhist Association and held that spot for two years. I made monthly reports on our income and expenses. I also budgeted for future events that our organization was planning on attending and finding fundraising opportunities. After that I was elected president of Jr. YBA. As president i held the monthly meetings and planned events.
